HEALTH TOPICS

The VOID provides fundamental knowledge about mental diseases, a variety of related issues, and the most recent findings in mental health research. The goal of the VOID is to educate users about their health and any problems they have been diagnosed with, not to provide them particular medical advice. For diagnosis, treatment, and personal inquiries, speak with a licenced healthcare professional.

DISCLAIMER: The VOID does not recommend any specific websites and just provides links to them for informative purposes.
